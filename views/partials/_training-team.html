Howdy, Y'all! I'm Janice. Did you know that dogs don't understand language? They understand body language much better than spoken words. We need to teach our dogs what we want them to do by using their bodies and their noses. If you want your dog to sit, for example, you would put your hand on their back and say "sit." You would also make sure that their back is nice and straight when they sit so that they feel comfortable in doing it correctly.
